Beautify Woodlawn

About us

Beautify Woodlawn is a group of volunteers who work together to improve the green spaces in the 300 block of Woodlawn Avenue. We plant flowers in tree wells, pull weeds in sidewalk cracks, help maintain the gardens at the clock and gazebo, and whatever else is needed to beautify this part of the neighborhood.

Join us!

There are two ways to get involved:

Adopt a spot

We are looking for volunteers to commit to adopt a small part of the sidewalk and street for six month terms. Adopters should be willing to visit their spot once a week to pick up litter, pull weeds, water plants, etc. Different levels of skill and time commitment are required for different spots.

Help out with a group project

We need people to help with occasional weeding and maintenance days at the gazebo and clock gardens at the corner of Woodlawn Ave. and Southern Parkway. Other opportunities to help improve our public space may pop up as well. If you’d like to receive email notifications about volunteer events, sign up here.
