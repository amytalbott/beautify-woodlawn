Beautify Woodlawn

Adopt a spot!

We are looking for volunteers to commit to overseeing a small part of the sidewalk and street for six months. After that, you will have the option to renew, or to have someone else take over that spot.

Plots are generally designated as one central feature (a tree or lighpost) and about 10 feet in each direction around that feature. Different levels of skill and time commitment are required for each type, so please look over these expectations and guidelines and choose the one that best fits you.
